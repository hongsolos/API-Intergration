CSCE 315 - 501 - Project 3

This project aim to familiarize students with the concept of web application. Using available APIs from big name company, the project help guide students in how to incorporate these services into their own design and usage. User feedbacks are then constructed to evaluate the quality of the product.

Topic: Where the heck is my bus?

Background: A&M bus system is a sostisphicated and often very overwhelming system. New and current students are often left waiting for a late bus without knowning what happen. This application will pull the students out of the dark and present somewhat of a situation report on the desired bus. Many but not all factors are considered, but the application should give an accurate guess on what is contributing to a late bus.

Factors:

  • Time and place a swap of drivers occur causing delay.
  • Twitter search on recent accidents.
  • Average time of the day the train pass that blocked a certain routes.
  • MAY BE IN EFFECT: Scanning of QR code to locate the bus.

Limitations:

  • Due to time constraints, only a few routes are implemented
  • Information gathering is limited.
